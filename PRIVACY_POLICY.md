# Privacy Policy - RoamQuest

**Last Updated**: February 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to RoamQuest ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our RoamQuest mobile application (the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use our App.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide to Us

**Account Information**
- **Email Address**: When you create an account, we collect your email address for authentication and communication purposes.
- **Apple Sign-In**: If you choose to sign in with Apple, we receive your email address and name from Apple.

**User-Generated Content**
- **Photos**: When you check in to locations, you may choose to take photos or select photos from your library. These photos are stored on our servers.
- **Location Data**: When you use check-in features, we may collect your current latitude and longitude coordinates.

**Travel Data**
- **Cities You Visit**: We track which cities you've explored to provide personalized recommendations.
- **Check-in History**: We maintain a record of places you've visited and checked off your list.

### 2. Information Automatically Collected

**Location Information**
- **Current Location**: We collect your precise location to detect your current city and generate personalized checklists.
- **Background Location**: The app may request location access when in use to provide city detection functionality.

**Device Information**
- **Device Identifier**: A unique identifier for your mobile device.
- **Device Type**: Model and operating system version.
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with the App, features used, and performance data.

### 3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

**Supabase (Backend Services)**
- Stores user accounts, checklists, and check-in data
- Hosts user photos
- [Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy)

**Apple (Sign In & In-App Purchase)**
- Provides authentication services
- Processes payments
- [Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/embedded-policy)

| Purpose | Description |

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

- **Encryption**: Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS.
- **Secure Storage**: User data is stored securely with access controls.
- **Authentication**: Password protection and optional biometric authentication.
- **Regular Audits**: We regularly review our security practices.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
